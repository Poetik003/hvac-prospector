Common Objections & Responses

Objection: "We're happy with our current provider"

Response: "That's great to hear! Many of our best clients were happy with their previous provider too. We found we could still help them reduce costs by 20-30% while improving reliability. Would you be interested in a no-obligation assessment?"

Objection: "We don't have budget right now"

Response: "I understand budget constraints. That's exactly why our program makes sense - it typically pays for itself through energy savings and avoiding emergency repairs. When would be a good time to revisit this?"

Objection: "Send me information"

Response: "I'd be happy to send information, but what I've found is that every facility is unique. A brief 15-minute facility walkthrough would let me provide recommendations specific to your operation. Are you available Tuesday or Wednesday?"
